Air Fryer Vegetable Egg Rolls

Air Fryer Egg Rolls are the most delicious vegan-friendly snack, appetizer or meal! Not fried, packed with healthy veggies and tofu and cooks in 8 minutes! Veggie Egg Rolls are the perfect healthy food everyone will love.

Ingredients

Makes 12 rolls - 127kcal/roll

  • Egg Roll Wrappers - 12
  • Shredded carrot - 3/4 cups
  • Finely sliced mushrooms - 1 1/4 cups
  • Bean sprouts - 2 cups
  • Sesame oil - 1 tbsp
  • Cabbage shredded - 3 cups
  • Block of firm tofu, crumbled - 400g
  • Cooking oil - 1 tbsp for brushing on rolls
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Tamari - 2 tbsp
  • Onion salt/powder - 1tbsp
  • Garlic powder - 1 tbsp
  • Rice Vinegar - 1 1/2 tbsp

Method

  1. Preheat a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sesame oil & crumbled tofu. Cook for 2-3 mins stirring often.
  3. Add in the carrots, mushrooms, beans sprouts and cabbage. Cook down for 2-3 minutes till cabbage is wilted.
  4. Add in tamari, spices, and rice vinegar. Stir till well combined and remove from heat.
  5. Assemble egg rolls (detailed description). Brush avocado oil or olive oil over the egg rolls. Brush the bottom of your air fryer basket with oil.
  6. Place egg rolls in your basket seam side down and not touching each other.
  7. Air Fry at 200 celsius for 8 minutes turning once at halfway.
